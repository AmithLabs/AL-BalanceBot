Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

AL BalanceBot is developed by AmithLabs. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

AL BalanceBot does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The application does not require user registration or login.

Bluetooth Communication

The application uses Bluetooth only for communication with compatible Arduino-based robots. Bluetooth communication is used for:

  • Robot control commands
  • PID tuning
  • Telemetry monitoring
  • Calibration commands

No Bluetooth data is transmitted to external servers.

Permissions

  • Bluetooth
  • Bluetooth Connect
  • Bluetooth Scan
  • Nearby Devices (Android 12+)

These permissions are required only for connecting to Bluetooth-enabled robots.

Data Sharing

AL BalanceBot does not sell, share or distribute user data.

Third-Party Services

This application does not use advertising or analytics services.
