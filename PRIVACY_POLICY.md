# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 2026

## AL BalanceBot Privacy Policy

AL BalanceBot ("the App") is developed by AmithLabs.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles your information.

## Information We Collect

AL BalanceBot does **not collect, store, or transmit any personal information**.

The app does not require user registration or login.

## Bluetooth Communication

The application uses Bluetooth only to communicate with compatible Arduino-based robots.

Bluetooth communication is used exclusively for:

- Sending robot control commands
- Receiving robot telemetry
- PID parameter configuration
- Calibration commands

No Bluetooth data is transmitted to external servers.

## Permissions

The application may request the following Android permissions:

- Bluetooth
- Bluetooth Connect
- Bluetooth Scan
- Nearby Devices (Android 12+)

These permissions are required solely for discovering and communicating with Bluetooth-enabled Arduino robots.

## Data Sharing

AL BalanceBot does not sell, share, or distribute user data.

## Third-Party Services

This application does not use third-party analytics or advertising services.

## Children's Privacy

The application is intended for educational and engineering purposes and does not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions of the application.
